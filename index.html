How It Works

Simple. Private. Precise.

Step 01

Order

Your kit is delivered to your door. Everything you need is inside.

Step 02

Swab

Swab the inside of your cheek at home. Takes two minutes. Completely painless.

Step 03

Post

Send your swab back in the prepaid envelope included in the kit. No clinic visit needed.

Step 04

Analysis

Your DNA is analysed in our EU-certified laboratory using ASPCR technology. 99.9% accuracy.

Step 05

Counsel & Report

Before you see a single number, a genetic counsellor walks you through your findings. Then your full report is yours — to keep, to share with your doctor, to act on.

Results delivered in 3 to 4 weeks from the day your sample reaches the lab.
