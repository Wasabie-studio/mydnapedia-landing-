About MyDNAPedia

We are not a DNA testing company. We are a wellness company that uses your DNA.

Most health advice is generic — built for an average person who doesn't exist. You eat clean, you train hard, you follow the same plan as everyone else, and somehow your results never match your effort. That's not a discipline problem. It's an information problem.

MyDNAPedia exists to close that gap. We were founded in Finland in 2012 on a simple belief: that your genes hold answers most doctors never test for. A decade later, having mapped over 1.8 million people globally, we brought that belief to India in 2020 — to give everyday people access to the kind of biological insight once reserved for elite athletes and high-level medical research.

The mission, in three words

Avoid. Reduce. Delay.

Every insight we deliver exists to help you avoid a condition you're predisposed to, reduce its severity if it appears, or delay its onset through informed action. Not fear. Not prediction. Just clarity — and a way forward.

Our Approach

How we think about your health.

01

Evidence-Based, Always

We communicate only what established, peer-reviewed science supports. We work in probabilities, not certainties — and we say "we don't know" when we genuinely don't. No trends. No overpromising.

02

Trust as Infrastructure

Every test is analysed in our EU-certified laboratory — 99.9% accuracy, the same Finland-based research standards used across Europe since 2012. Your data is encrypted, confidential, and never used beyond what you've explicitly agreed to.

03

Empowerment, Never Fear

The feeling we aim for is relief, not alarm. When you get your results, the response should be: "Now I know what I can do" — not anxiety. Every insight comes paired with an action, never a risk left standing alone.

04

A Lifelong Relationship, Not a Transaction

Our role doesn't end when your report is delivered. Counselling, follow-up, and ongoing guidance are core to what we do — because a transformed customer matters more to us than a satisfied one.
